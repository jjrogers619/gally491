During evening hours, the Rathskellar transforms its menu to that of a pub with various beers, wines and beverage options. Monday through Saturday, the Rathskellar is an excellent space to have a quick bite during dinner and late night hours. Meal plan equivalencies are available during all business operating hours. A la carte options are available after 2pm. Accepting credit cards, dining dollars and cash transactions for those on the go.
